## What is Power BI

- Business Intelligence software from Microsoft
- Not just a visualisation tool
  - Emphasises the cleaning (PowerQuery) and calculations (Data Analysis Expressions: DAX), before the visualisations
    - The same functionality is built into Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, Power View
- There is the Deskop app (free, where you prepare the data and visualisation) and the Service (where you share the data)
